Rajasthan Tourism

Rajasthan is undoubtedly one of the most colourful places on earth. From gold to blue and white to pink, you will find cities in different hues that will awaken your senses. Each city in the largest state in India is beautiful and unique in its own way, with plenty to offer. Some of the unmissable cities to visit in Rajasthan include the Golden City of Jaisalmer, the Pink City of Jaipur, the Blue City of Jodhpur, the Holy City of Ajmer and the City of Lakes, Udaipur.
